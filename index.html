Session One  ·  An Acoustic Session

Foo Fighters

The songs, acoustic, under the dome.

This is not the band. It is their music, played acoustic by one musician with one guitar, stripped of stadium production and held in a room of two hundred. The dome goes black, the sky opens, and the songs are returned to you.

The room.

Two hundred seats. One candle on stage for every seat occupied.

NightSky Sessions is an acoustic concert series performed inside TELUS World of Science - Edmonton. Iconic songs, stripped of stadium production, played by one musician with one guitar, under a projected sky of stars, aurora, and mountains.

The candles are placed before doors open, never announced. The room settles into stillness before the first note.

No opening act. No merch table. No DJ. No noise.

What the night holds.

One performer  ·  One guitar  ·  The sky above you

One electronic candle on stage for every seat occupied. Placed before doors. Never explained.

Under the dome

A projected night sky of stars, aurora, mountain ridgelines, and shooting stars overhead.

The soundscape

Crickets, frogs, and crackling fire fill the lobby and carry through the room.

A setlist card
